The ratios of profitability analysis
Ratio Result % Explanation
Net Profit Margin or rate / return on sales {{ net_profit_margin }} This ratio measures the net profit achieved by each pound of net sales.
Gross Profit margin {{ gross_profit_margin }} Measures the gross profit achieved by each by each bound of net sales
Total asset turnover {{ total_assets_turnover }} Measures the ability of the firm to generate net sales through the active use of assets in different operations of the firm.
Rate of return on assets/ Return on Investment {{ rate_of_return_on_assets }} Measures net profit achieved by each pound of total assets
Rate of return on total equity {{ rate_of_return_on_total_equity}} Measures the ability of Owner's equity to generate net profit
