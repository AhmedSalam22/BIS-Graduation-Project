The ratios of liquidity analysis
Ratio Result Explanation
Current ratio {{ current_ratio|floatformat:2 }} This ratio measures the ability of Current assets for paying current liabilities. This ratio may be good or bad according to the ability of inventory to convert quickly to cash.
Acid-test ratio {{ acid_test_ratio|floatformat:2 }} This ratio measures the ability of most liquid assets to pay the short-term liabilities
Quick ratio {{ quick_ratio|floatformat:2 }} This ratio measures the ability of most liquid assets to pay the short-term liabilities
Cash ratio {{ cash_ratio|floatformat:2 }} This ratio measures the ability of cash for paying the short-term liabilities
